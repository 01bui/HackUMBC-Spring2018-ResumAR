CAN YOU IMAGINE OUR WORLD WITH NO AUGMENTED REALITY?

Of course, you can. It’s simple! But your kids probably can’t—or, at least, they don’t want to! For children today, their AR world is a great place to discover and enjoy. Augmented reality is fun—and causes no harm. Kids love their smartphones and tablets—but they still love playing with toys. Augmented reality gives them the best of both worlds.

AR Christmas Card

Feel Christmas magic with a simple Augmented Reality app. The card was designed to share Christmas joy with all IT Craft’s clients and friends. The card contains a QR code with a link to a special Christmas app and a picture that activated it. Upon scanning the picture, a 3D snow globe appears on a smartphone screen and music starts to play.

AR for Publishers

A demo app designed to show how Augmented Reality technology works with book illustrations. Any illustration can become an AR marker: when pointing at it, a 3D model activates. Users can interact with it—zoom, rotate or view from different angles. Also, different sounds are possible as well as snapshots to be shared with friends or posted in social networks.

View work

AR Toy models

This demo app was presented on Nuremberg Toy Fair to show the many opportunities Augmented Reality apps offer toy manufacturers. The demo app lets visitors play around with a 3D model of a starship when pointing with a camera on its image. The app was used to illustrate the basics of Augmented Reality mechanics and demonstrate how potential users can play with a new Augmented Reality toy.

Augmented reality toys

Augmented reality toys combine the best of both worlds—the digital world and the world of real toys—boosting kids’ creativity and encouraging them to learn more. They can make something and literally hold it in their hands. They can quickly make changes to what they’ve already done and thrill to see a result without fearing they might ruin something. They learn how things work. This all makes augmented reality the best tool to introduce new discoveries that open doors to their imagination. Toys and games open these doors!
